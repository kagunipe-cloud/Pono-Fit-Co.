**Account deletion (Guideline 5.1.1(v))**

Members can delete their account entirely from within the **Pono Fit** iOS app:

1. Sign in as a member.
2. Open **Profile** (from the member home / navigation).
3. Scroll to **Delete account** at the bottom of the profile screen.
4. Enter the account **password** and confirm.

This flow is **only available in the native app** (not the staff web dashboard). A password is required to confirm deletion.

**After deletion:** We revoke facility door access (Kisi). If the member has no purchase/subscription/booking history, the member record is removed. If they have history we must retain for business or legal purposes, we close the login and remove personal profile details while retaining anonymized/operational records as described in our Privacy Policy.

**Privacy Policy URL:** `https://beponofitco.com/privacy` — Section 8 (“Deleting your account (mobile app)”) describes the same process and data handling.
